Netra App

Netra is an Android App built for KMIT students.
From Netra App they have access to all the details that they need to know while they are at KMIT.
From Netra App students can not only cross-check
  1. The Attendance

  2. time-table

  3. performance

  4. events and results

  5. But also access Conversations- Technical and General videos built by KMIT to help students stay updated with the latest in the industry.


Students also receive notifications from KMIT, in cases like class cancellations
